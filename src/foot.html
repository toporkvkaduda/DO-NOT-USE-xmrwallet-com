XMRWallet.com is a free web-based lightweight wallet, enabling you to send and receive Monero without having to download any software. It is open-source, which means anyone can see the code and help make it better. Plus, you do not need to register or give any of your personal information to use it.

XMRWallet.com does not keep any records of your transactions. You can use it without worrying about anyone seeing what you are doing with your money.
