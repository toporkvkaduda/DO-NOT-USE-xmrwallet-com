How To Use XMRWallet
XMRWallet is a web-based interface. It helps you use Monero without running a full Monero node. Using XMRWallet for large amounts you may have considerable risk. We recommend you treat XMRWallet like you will treat your actual wallet. Try to not store large amounts in it. If you would like to use Monero for long-term storage, we suggest creating an account, storing your private key in a safe place and only use it once.
You must save your Seed.
Above this information you will find your "Seed" of twenty five words. You must keep it secure and private. Remember that it is very important for you. This is the only way for you to login to your Monero account. We cannot store your "Seed" on the server and if you lost it we will not have opportunity to recover it. You cannot change your "Seed". If it is stolen you must move your funds to a new account with a new "Seed". The best way to backup your "Seed" is by writing it down on a piece of paper, laminating it and storing it in a secure place.
