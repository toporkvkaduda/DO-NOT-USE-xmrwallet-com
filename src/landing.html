Security of Monero
Monero is a secure digital cash which is operated by a user's network. Monero is also a decentralized cryptocurrency. All transactions are confirmed by distributed consensus and all transactions then recorded to the blockchain immutably. You need not to trust any third-parties to keep your Monero safe.
Monero is untraceable
Monero obfuscates all receiving and sending address and all transacted amounts as well, no one can link the Monero blockchain to a particular user or make a real-world identity match.
Privacy of Monero
To obfuscate the destinations origins and amounts of all transactions Monero uses stealth address, ring confidential transactions and ring signature. All the benefits of a decentralized cryptocurrency are provided by Monero with no typical privacy concessions.
Changeable Monero
Monero is changeable because it is private by default. Vendors cant blacklist Monero or exchange it due to the association with previous transactions.
